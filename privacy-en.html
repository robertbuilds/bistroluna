Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 January 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this website collects, uses, and protects data submitted by visitors. Our goal is to ensure transparency and compliance with applicable data protection laws (GDPR). This policy applies to all pages of the restaurant website, including About Us, Menu, Gallery, Reservations, and Contact.

2. Data Collection

We only collect data that you voluntarily provide through forms on the website:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Reservation details (date, time, number of guests, special requests)
  • Messages or inquiries submitted via the contact form

No sensitive data is collected, and we do not share your information with third parties, except as needed to process reservations (e.g., with third-party booking platforms if used).

3. Purpose of Data Collection

The collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Processing and confirming reservations.
  • Responding to inquiries or questions sent via the contact form.
  • Maintaining effective communication with site visitors.
  • Sending promotional offers or updates only if you opt in explicitly.

4. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

5. User Rights

Visitors have the right to:

  • Access their personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of their data.
  • Restrict processing or withdraw consent.
  • Contact the site administrator regarding any questions about personal data.

6. Data Retention

Data submitted via the contact or reservation forms is kept only for the time necessary to respond to the inquiry or process the reservation and is deleted afterward.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be available on this page. Visitors are encouraged to check this page regularly.
